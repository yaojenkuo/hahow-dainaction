Data Analyst 09/14更新

台灣多耐福股份有限公司 本公司其他工作
應徵
30人以上應徵

工作內容

Role: We are seeking a skilled and self-motivated Data Analyst to join our team. The ideal candidate will possess strong analytical skills, a basic understanding of manufacturing business, and the ability to work with large complex sales and manufacturing data. The Data Analyst will collaborate with stakeholders to gather business demands, analyze data, build statistical models and provide valuable insights to support our digital transformation targets. Responsibilities: • Conduct statistical data modelling and analysis related to Sales, Production, Logistics functions. • Discover potential demands and translate requirements into data-driven solutions with stakeholders. • Utilize Python programming and machine learning techniques to build analytical models. • Interpret data and present findings to stakeholders, identify opportunities for process optimization and offer data-driven insights. • Work closely with data engineer, apps developers to develop whole data analytics system including end-to-end analytical pipeline and MLops. • Work closely with data team to promote digital transformation. Requirements: • Academic degree in Data Science, Statistics, Computer Science or a related field. • Prefer analytical experience in Manufacturing, Sales, Logistics field, but graduated is acceptable. • Knowledge of machine learning, deep learning and Linux system. • Experience of machine learning including linear algorithms, ensemble learning, statistical referencing. • Experience of model fine-tuning, model performance validation and auto parameter-tuning. • Proficiency in Python, SQL languages and Gitlab workflow. • Experience of using big data framework such as Spark, Flink would be a plus. • Experience of deep learning modelling in computer vision would be a plus. • Experience of using Gitlab CI/CD, Docker, K8s would be a plus. Character: • Strong sense of responsibility and a good team player; • Good communication skills including spoken, written English and Chinese;
