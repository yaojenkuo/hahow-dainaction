1. Data mining and analysis - Developing and implementing data analyses, data collection systems and other strategies that optimize statistical efficiency and quality. - Build, develop and maintain data models, reporting systems, data automation systems, dashboards and performance metrics that support key business decisions. - Clearly communicating the findings from the analysis to turn information into action through visualized /graphic reports, dashboards, and/or presentations. - Preparing KPI report and continuously tracking campaign performance evaluation. 2. Business Strategy and Sales - Product category/ segmentation/ behavior analysis to explore member insights and opportunity to increase sales through using various techniques and approaches. - Understanding the business requirements, through using model/ segmentation data analysis to explore the fact to answer specific business answers/requirement. - Using A/B testing model to apply to each campaign and quantified valuable result and sales. - Keeping up to date with knowledge of CRM Data Analytics trends and advising on new approaches to adopt. 3. Cross function Communication - Working closely with cross-functional teams to ensure effective communication and coordination of CRM Data Analytics initiatives. - Cross function data analysis (product view, sales view) support for seek uncover opportunities. 4. BI reporting and CRM platform enhancement - CRM report BI tools and report build up for member performance/ sales performance/ campaign evaluation automatically. - CRM campaign automation platform management and identify areas to increase efficiency and automation of processes
